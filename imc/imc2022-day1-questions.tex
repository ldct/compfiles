Problem 2. Let n be a positive integer. Find all n×n real matrices A with only real eigenvalues
satisfying
                                        A + Ak = AT
for some integer k ≥ n.
(AT denotes the transpose of A.)

Problem 3. Let p be a prime number. A flea is staying at point 0 of the real line. At each
minute, the flea has three possibilities: to stay at its position, or to move by 1 to the left or
to the right. After p − 1 minutes, it wants to be at 0 again. Denote by f (p) the number of its
strategies to do this (for example, f (3) = 3: it may either stay at 0 for the entire time, or go
to the left and then to the right, or go to the right and then to the left). Find f (p) modulo p.

                                                                                     (10 points)

\section*{Problem 1}
Let $(a_n), (b_n)$ be sequences of positive numbers. Show the equivalence of:
\begin{itemize}
\item[(1)] There exists a sequence $(c_n)$ of positive numbers such that $\sum a_n/c_n$ and $\sum c_n/b_n$ both converge.
\item[(2)] $\sum \sqrt{a_n/b_n}$ converges.
\end{itemize}

\section*{Problem 2}
Does there exist a field whose multiplicative group is isomorphic to its additive group?

\section*{Problem 3}
Determine all rational $a$ for which the matrix
\[ \begin{pmatrix} a & -a & -1 & 0 \\ a & -a & 0 & -1 \\ 1 & 0 & a & -a \\ 0 & 1 & a & -a \end{pmatrix} \]
is the square of a matrix with rational entries.

\section*{Problem 4}
Find all differentiable $f : (0, \infty) \to \mathbb{R}$ such that
\[ f(b) - f(a) = (b - a) f'(\sqrt{ab}) \quad \text{for all } a, b > 0. \]

\section*{Problem 5}
Let $p < q$ be primes. In a convex polygon $P_1 P_2 \cdots P_{pq}$ all angles are equal and side lengths are distinct positive integers. Prove that $P_1 P_2 + P_2 P_3 + \cdots + P_k P_{k+1} \geq (k^3 + k)/2$ for $1 \leq k \leq p$.

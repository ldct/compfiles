\section*{Problem 6}
Let $z$ be a complex number with $|z+1| > 2$. Prove that $|z^3 + 1| > 1$.

\section*{Problem 7}
Let $p$ and $q$ be relatively prime positive integers. Prove that
\[ \sum_{k=0}^{pq-1}(-1)^{\lfloor k/p \rfloor + \lfloor k/q \rfloor} = \begin{cases} 0 & \text{if } pq \text{ is even} \\ 1 & \text{if } pq \text{ is odd.} \end{cases} \]

\section*{Problem 8}
Suppose that $v_1, \dots, v_d$ are unit vectors in $\mathbb{R}^d$. Prove that there exists a unit vector $u$ such that $|u \cdot v_i| \le 1/\sqrt{d}$ for $i = 1, \dots, d$.

\section*{Problem 9}
Does there exist an infinite set $M$ of positive integers such that for any $a, b \in M$ with $a < b$, the sum $a + b$ is square-free?

\section*{Problem 10}
Consider a circular necklace with $2013$ beads, each white or green. A painting is \emph{good} if among any $21$ successive beads there is at least one green bead. Prove that the number of good paintings is odd. (Rotations/reflections give distinct paintings.)

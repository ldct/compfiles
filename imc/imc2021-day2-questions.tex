Problem 6. For a prime number p, let GL2 (Z/pZ) be the group of invertible 2 × 2 matrices of
residues modulo p, and let Sp be the symmetric group (the group of all permutations) on p elements.
Show that there is no injective group homomorphism ϕ ∶ GL2 (Z/pZ) → Sp .
                                                                                        (10 points)

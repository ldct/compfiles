Problem 9. We say that a real number V is good if there exist two closed convex subsets X,
Y of the unit cube in R3 , with volume V each, such that for each of the three coordinate planes
(that is, the planes spanned by any two of the three coordinate axes), the projections of X and
Y onto that plane are disjoint.
   Find sup{V | V is good}.
